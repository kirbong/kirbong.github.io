About

Welcome! My name is Kristen Marcinek

I am a graduate of Rutgers University - New Brunswick with a B.A. in Information Technology and Informatics. Currently, I am a Game Design MA candidate at American University, as well as a Museum Experience Associate with the National Children's Museum. I am also the former VP and a founding member of Rutgers Women's Gaming League. I'm interested in game dev and design and have taken coursework on game ideation, Unity, and C#!
